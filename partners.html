Our Partners

Advancing inclusive development through strong multi-stakeholder partnerships.

Flagship Institutional Partner

Network for Fish Quality Management & Sustainable Fishing (NETFISH)

(MPEDA, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India)

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a proud implementing NGO partner and national level network member (Reg. No. 2) for the Network for Fish Quality Management and Sustainable Fishing (NETFISH). Our strategic partnership has been established since 2008, representing over 15 years of dedicated collaboration in sustainable coastal development.

Working alongside Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs), local governments, and line departments across Odisha—primarily in Balasore, Jagatsinghpur, and Bhadrak—SRMSS executes critical programmes with annual financial assistance from NETFISH.

Core Collaborative Programmes

  • Capacity Building: Training fisher communities, PRIs, and government officials on sustainable fisheries management, coastal governance, and fish quality management.
  • Conservation: Educating stakeholders to protect endangered species, adopt sustainable fishery practices, and maintain vital marine ecosystem balance.
  • Coastal Livelihoods: Promoting fishery-based sustainable livelihoods, allied agriculture practices, skill development for income generation, and facilitating market linkages.
  • Community Resilience: Implementing comprehensive disaster preparedness and recovery activities to minimize natural disaster impacts on coastal communities.

Official Letter of Recommendation

"We experienced that the organization operates with great accountability, transparency, and credibility; adhering to government guidelines at all levels while implementing the programmes. NETFISH strongly recommends the government, donor agencies, and other institutions provide financial assistance to SRMSS... Their professionalism, expertise, and dedication make them an ideal partner for any agency."

— Dr. Joice V Thomas, Chief Executive, NETFISH (MPEDA)

Broader Network

Working Hand-in-Hand With Stakeholders

In addition to our flagship institutional partners, SRMSS closely collaborates with local communities, PRIs, and development agencies to address issues through practical, grassroots initiatives.

Community Institutions & SHGs

We collaborate directly with traditional fisher groups, women's Self-Help Groups (SHGs), and Village Disaster Management Committees to foster participatory local governance and ownership.

Government & PRIs

Partnering with government health departments, local authorities, and Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs) to improve access to welfare schemes, health referral networks, and WASH services.

Academic & Research

Working closely with educational institutions, scientific researchers, and conservation partners to conduct habitat mapping, biodiversity monitoring, and horseshoe crab protection.

SRMSS

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a registered grassroots non-profit organization dedicated to women empowerment, coastal conservation, and participatory development.
