Our Partners

Advancing inclusive development through strong multi-stakeholder partnerships.

Flagship Institutional Partner

Network for Fish Quality Management & Sustainable Fishing (NETFISH)

(MPEDA, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India)

[span_0](start_span)

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a proud implementing NGO partner and national level network member (Reg. No. 2) for the Network for Fish Quality Management and Sustainable Fishing (NETFISH)[span_0](end_span). [span_1](start_span)[span_2](start_span)Our strategic partnership has been established since 2008, representing over 15 years of dedicated collaboration in sustainable coastal development[span_1](end_span)[span_2](end_span).

[span_3](start_span)[span_4](start_span)

Working alongside Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs), local governments, and line departments across Odisha—primarily in Balasore, Jagatsinghpur, and Bhadrak—SRMSS executes critical programmes with annual financial assistance from NETFISH[span_3](end_span)[span_4](end_span).

Core Collaborative Programmes

    [span_5](start_span)[span_6](start_span)
  • Capacity Building: Training fisher communities, PRIs, and government officials on sustainable fisheries management, coastal governance, and fish quality management[span_5](end_span)[span_6](end_span).
    • [span_7](start_span)[span_8](start_span)[span_9](start_span)
  • Conservation: Educating stakeholders to protect endangered species, adopt sustainable fishery practices, and maintain vital marine ecosystem balance[span_7](end_span)[span_8](end_span)[span_9](end_span).
    • [span_10](start_span)[span_11](start_span)
  • Coastal Livelihoods: Promoting fishery-based sustainable livelihoods, allied agriculture practices, skill development for income generation, and facilitating market linkages[span_10](end_span)[span_11](end_span).
    • [span_12](start_span)
  • Community Resilience: Implementing comprehensive disaster preparedness and recovery activities to minimize natural disaster impacts on coastal communities[span_12](end_span).
    • [span_13](start_span)
  • Cross-Cutting Development: Addressing holistic community needs including health, nutrition, hygiene, education, and women empowerment[span_13](end_span).

Broader Network

Working Hand-in-Hand With Stakeholders

In addition to our flagship institutional partners, SRMSS closely collaborates with local communities, PRIs, and development agencies to address issues through practical, grassroots initiatives.

Community Institutions & SHGs

We collaborate directly with traditional fisher groups, women's Self-Help Groups (SHGs), and Village Disaster Management Committees to foster participatory local governance and ownership.

Government & PRIs

Partnering with government health departments, local authorities, and Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs) to improve access to welfare schemes, health referral networks, and WASH services.

Academic & Research Bodies

Working closely with educational institutions, scientific researchers, and conservation partners to conduct habitat mapping, biodiversity monitoring, and horseshoe crab protection.

SRMSS

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a registered grassroots non-profit organization dedicated to women empowerment, coastal conservation, and participatory development.
