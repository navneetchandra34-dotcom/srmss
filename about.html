Our Origins

Rooted in Grassroots Action

[span_40](start_span)

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a registered grassroots non-profit organization established in 2007 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, in Odisha by Late Jyotna Rani Jena[span_40](end_span).

[span_41](start_span)

SRMSS began as a community-based initiative to respond to the challenges faced by women, children, fishing communities, and other vulnerable groups[span_41](end_span). [span_42](start_span)Over the years, the organization has expanded its work across several districts of Odisha while maintaining its strong grassroots presence[span_42](end_span).

[span_43](start_span)

The organization was formed with the understanding that development is most effective when local communities are involved in identifying their priorities and taking part in the solutions[span_43](end_span).

Our Vision

[span_45](start_span)

A just, inclusive, and resilient society where every person, especially women, children, and marginalized communities, has the opportunity to live with dignity, realize their potential, and contribute to sustainable and equitable development[span_45](end_span).

Our Mission

[span_46](start_span)

To strengthen the capacities of communities through participatory development, promote gender equality and sustainable livelihoods, improve access to health and essential services, conserve natural resources, and promote partnerships that advance inclusive and environmentally sustainable development[span_46](end_span).
