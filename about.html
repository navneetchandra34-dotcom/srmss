About Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti

A registered grassroots non-profit dedicated to inclusive growth across Odisha since 2007.

Our Origins

Rooted in Grassroots Action

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a registered grassroots non-profit organization formally established in 2007 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, in Odisha by Late Jyotna Rani Jena.

The organization began as a targeted community-based initiative to respond directly to the socioeconomic and environmental challenges faced by women, children, traditional fishing communities, and vulnerable rural groups.

We collaborate closely with women's groups, government departments, Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs), and development agencies to address local issues through practical, community-driven execution.

Our Guiding Principle

"Development is most effective when local communities are directly involved in identifying their priorities and taking part in the solutions."

Vision

A just, inclusive, and resilient society where every person, especially women, children, and marginalized communities, has the opportunity to live with dignity, realize their potential, and contribute to sustainable and equitable development.

Mission

To strengthen the capacities of communities through participatory development, promote gender equality and sustainable livelihoods, improve access to health and essential services, and conserve natural resources.
