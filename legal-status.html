Governance & Compliance

Commitment to Transparency

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) operates as a registered grassroots non-profit organization formally established in 2007 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, in Odisha by Late Jyotna Rani Jena.

Since its inception, the organization has maintained adherence to statutory regulations while dedicating its resources to community-driven development, natural resource conservation, and women empowerment across vulnerable rural and coastal areas of Odisha.

All programmatic partnerships and institutional activities are conducted in accordance with the rules of the Societies Registration Act, 1860.
