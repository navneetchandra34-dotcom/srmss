Commitment to Transparency

[span_163](start_span)

Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) operates as a registered grassroots non-profit organization formally established in 2007 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, in Odisha by Late Jyotna Rani Jena[span_163](end_span).

[span_164](start_span)

Since its establishment, the organization has maintained adherence to statutory regulations while dedicating its resources to community-driven development, natural resource conservation, and women empowerment across vulnerable rural and coastal areas of Odisha[span_164](end_span).

[span_165](start_span)

All programmatic partnerships and institutional activities are conducted in accordance with the rules of the Societies Registration Act, 1860[span_165](end_span).
