Commitment to Transparency

Since its inception in 2007, Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti has maintained strict adherence to the statutory regulations of the Government of India.

We are fully compliant with national tax laws, registered on the NITI Aayog NGO Darpan portal, and eligible for corporate grants through our CSR-1 registration. Our dedication to financial transparency ensures that every resource is effectively deployed toward community-driven development.
