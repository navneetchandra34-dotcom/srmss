Subha Rashmi Mahila Seva Samiti (SRMSS) is a registered grassroots non-profit organization established in 2007 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, in Odisha by Late Jyotna Rani Jena[span_30](end_span).

Community-Driven Action

Our Mission & Reach

[span_31](start_span)

We work with rural and coastal communities to improve livelihoods, conserve natural resources, strengthen local institutions, and increase access to basic services for vulnerable households[span_31](end_span).

Women Leadership

[span_32](start_span)

We support women to build strong Self-Help Groups (SHGs), develop livelihood skills, start small enterprises, and improve financial literacy[span_32](end_span).
